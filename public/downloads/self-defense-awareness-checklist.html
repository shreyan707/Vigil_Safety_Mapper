VIGIL Checklist

Self-Defense Awareness Checklist

This checklist focuses on prevention, exit planning, and simple last-resort actions that create space to get away.

Before you travel

  • Charge your phone and keep emergency numbers in favourites.
  • Share your route or live location with a trusted person if needed.
  • Choose well-lit public routes when possible.
  • Avoid blocking both ears with audio in unfamiliar spaces.

When something feels wrong

  • Trust early discomfort and change direction quickly.
  • Move toward crowds, shops, transport staff, or security.
  • Call someone out loud so others know you need attention.
  • Keep distance and keep an exit in view.

Use your voice

  • Say stop, back away, or call the police in a loud, direct tone.
  • Repeat the command while moving toward safety.
  • Do not worry about being polite when your safety is at risk.

If escape is blocked

  • Create enough space to run rather than staying to fight.
  • Use simple strikes only to break contact and leave.
  • Report details quickly once you reach safety.
  • Preserve clothing, photos, and messages if a crime occurred.
